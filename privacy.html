Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Information we collect

We may collect the information you provide when you contact us or place an order, including your name, phone number, email address, delivery details, order details and message content.

How we use information

  • Respond to enquiries and fulfil orders.
  • Provide customer support and resolve order issues.
  • Improve our menu, service and website.
  • Meet legal and operational requirements.

Sharing and storage

We only share personal information with service providers needed to operate Food2Suit, such as hosting, payment, delivery or email providers. We keep information only for as long as reasonably needed for the purposes above or legal requirements.

Your choices

You may ask to access, correct or delete personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable law. Contact us through our contact page.

Cookies and third-party services

We currently use necessary browser storage to remember site preferences and tray contents. If we add analytics, advertising or other non-essential cookies, this policy and cookie controls will be updated.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Contact and complaints

Contact Food2Suit first with privacy questions. You may also have rights under Ghana’s Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843).

This is a practical website draft and should be reviewed by a qualified Ghanaian legal professional before launch.
